Windows

Microsoft® Windows® 8, 7, Vista, XP, 2008, 2003, o 2000
Procesador Pentium compatible
1 GB de memoria interna
45 MB de espacio libre en disco

  • Adobe Photoshop CS4 (64-bit y 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS3, CS2, CS, 7
  • Adobe Photoshop Elements 11, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6
El plug-in de Exportación de PhotoZoom Pro 5 es compatible con:
  • Adobe Photoshop CS6 (64-bit y 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5.5 (64-bit y 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5 (64-bit y 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS4 (64-bit y 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS3, CS2, CS, 7
  • Adobe Photoshop Elements 11, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2
  • Corel PaintShop Pro X5
  • Corel PaintShop Pro X4
  • Corel PaintShop Photo Pro X3
  • Corel Paint Shop Pro Photo X2
  • Corel Paint Shop Pro Photo XI
  • Corel Paint Shop Pro X
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X6 (64-bit y 32-bit)
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X5
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X4
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X3
y también debería de funcionar con otros programas que son compatibles con los plug-ins de Adobe Photoshop.
