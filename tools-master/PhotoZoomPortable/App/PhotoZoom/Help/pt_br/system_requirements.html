Requisitos de Sistema


Windows

Microsoft® Windows® 8, 7, Vista, XP, 2008, 2003, or 2000
Processador compatível com o Pentium.
1 GB internal memory
45 MB de espaço livre no disco rígido

O plug-in de Automatização do PhotoZoom Pro 5 é compatível com:

  • Adobe Photoshop CS6 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5.5 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS4 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS3, CS2, CS, 7
  • Adobe Photoshop Elements 11, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6
O plug-in de Exportação do PhotoZoom Pro 5 é compatível com:
  • Adobe Photoshop CS6 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5.5 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS4 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS3, CS2, CS, 7
  • Adobe Photoshop Elements 11, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2
  • Corel PaintShop Pro X5
  • Corel PaintShop Pro X4
  • Corel PaintShop Photo Pro X3
  • Corel Paint Shop Pro Photo X2
  • Corel Paint Shop Pro Photo XI
  • Corel Paint Shop Pro X
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X6 (64-bit e 32-bit)
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X5
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X4
  • Corel PHOTO-PAINT X3
e deverá também funcionar com outros programas compatíveis com os plug-ins do Adobe Photoshop.


Mac

OS X 10.8 (Mountain Lion), 10.7 (Lion), 10.6 (Snow Leopard), or 10.5 (Leopard)
Processador Intel ou PowerPC G4/G5
1 GB internal memory
60 MB de espaço livre no disco rígido

Ambos os plug-ins de Automatização e de Exportação do PhotoZoom Pro 5 são compatíveis com:

  • Adobe Photoshop CS6
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5.5 (64-bit and 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS5 (64-bit and 32-bit)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS4 (Universal)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS3 (Universal)
  • Adobe Photoshop CS2 9.0.2
  • Adobe Photoshop Elements 11, 10, 9, 8, 6
e deverá também funcionar com outros programas compatíveis com os plug-ins do Adobe Photoshop.
